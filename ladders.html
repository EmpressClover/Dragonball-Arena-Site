Player Ladder

The Player Ladder is a game-wide ladder which everyone automatically joins when they begin playing Dragon Ball Arena. It shows a player's individual skill in their quest of becoming the Zen Oh.

Clan Ladder

The Clan Ladder is a ladder where everyone automatically joins when they join or create a clan. It shows a clan's skill and ability to work together.
